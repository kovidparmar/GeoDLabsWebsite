About Our Team

Our lab brings together experts from various disciplines including geospatial science, computer science, environmental science, and urban planning. This interdisciplinary approach allows us to tackle complex problems with innovative perspectives and methodologies. Our team members come from diverse backgrounds and are united by a passion for using geospatial technology to address real-world challenges.

Dr. Sharma specializes in remote sensing applications for urban studies and has led multiple national and international research projects. His current work focuses on developing machine learning approaches for analyzing multi-temporal satellite imagery.
