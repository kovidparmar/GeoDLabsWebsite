Advancing Geospatial Science

GeoD Labs is a premier research center at IIT Gandhinagar focused on innovative applications of geospatial data science to solve complex real-world challenges.

Our Mission

GeoD Labs is dedicated to advancing research in geospatial data science and technology. We develop innovative approaches to analyze, visualize, and interpret complex geospatial data for solving real-world problems in domains such as urban planning, environmental monitoring, disaster management, and sustainable development.

Our Vision

We envision a future where cutting-edge geospatial technologies and data science methodologies are seamlessly integrated to address critical global challenges. Our lab aims to be at the forefront of this integration, producing impactful research and training the next generation of geospatial data scientists and engineers who will shape the future of our planet.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in our research and education, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in geospatial science.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking and novel approaches to complex problems, fostering an environment where new ideas can flourish.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of interdisciplinary teamwork and partnerships to tackle the most challenging problems in our field.

Sustainability

Our History & Achievements

A journey of growth and excellence in geospatial research

2018

Establishment of GeoD Labs

2018

Founded by Dr. Anand Sharma with a focus on applying advanced geospatial technologies to environmental challenges in India. The lab began with three researchers and a mission to transform how we understand and interact with spatial data.

2019

First Major Research Grant

2019

Received â‚¹2.5 crore national funding for developing remote sensing applications for urban planning in rapidly growing Indian cities. This landmark project established our methodology for integrating multiple data sources for comprehensive urban analysis.

2020

Expansion of Research Focus

2020

Incorporated artificial intelligence and machine learning methodologies into our geospatial research framework. Our pioneering work on deep learning for satellite imagery analysis set new standards in the field.

2021

International Recognition

2021

Our work on flood risk assessment using integrated remote sensing techniques received international acclaim, winning the Best Paper Award at the International Conference on Geospatial Technologies (ICGT 2021).

2022

Lab Expansion

2022

Added new advanced computing infrastructure and expanded our team to include more researchers and students. Our state-of-the-art GPU cluster became one of the most powerful computing resources for geospatial analytics in the country.

2023

International Collaborations

2023

Established formal research partnerships with leading universities in USA, UK, and Australia. These collaborations have led to joint research projects, student exchanges, and global knowledge sharing initiatives.

2024

Launch of Open Data Initiative

2024

Started releasing our research datasets to the public to foster collaboration and innovation in the field. Our India Urban Growth Database became the definitive resource for urban planning research in the region.

2025

Center of Excellence

2025

Recognized as a National Center of Excellence in Geospatial Data Science by the Government of India, affirming our position as a leader in advanced geospatial research and education in the country.
