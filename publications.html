Featured Publication

Machine Learning Approaches for Urban Growth Prediction Using Multi-temporal Satellite Imagery

This groundbreaking study introduces a novel deep learning architecture that combines convolutional neural networks with time series analysis to accurately predict urban expansion patterns in rapidly growing Indian cities. Using multi-temporal Landsat and Sentinel imagery spanning two decades, our model achieves prediction accuracy of 92.7%, significantly outperforming traditional methods.

Published in International Journal of Remote Sensing (Impact Factor: 4.23)
