Remote Sensing & Earth Observation

Our lab specializes in developing advanced methodologies for processing and analyzing multi-source and multi-temporal remote sensing data to monitor environmental changes, urban growth patterns, and land use dynamics. We work with various satellite platforms including optical, radar, and hyperspectral sensors to extract meaningful information for environmental monitoring and decision making.

Key Research Topics:

  • Multi-temporal satellite image analysis for land cover/land use change detection
  • Integration of optical and radar data for improved feature extraction
  • Hyperspectral image processing for detailed surface characterization
  • Development of automated methods for large-scale satellite data processing
  • Application of remote sensing for environmental monitoring

Featured Projects:

Urban Growth Monitoring System

Developing an automated system to track urban expansion patterns across major Indian cities using time-series satellite imagery and deep learning approaches.

Spatial Analysis & Modeling

Our research in spatial analysis focuses on developing and applying advanced analytical methods to understand spatial patterns, relationships, and processes. We work on creating innovative spatial statistical approaches, simulation models, and analytical frameworks to address complex spatial problems in various domains.

Key Research Topics:

  • Spatial statistical methods for pattern detection and analysis
  • Agent-based modeling for simulating spatial processes
  • Network analysis for transportation and infrastructure systems
  • Spatio-temporal modeling of environmental and socioeconomic phenomena
  • Uncertainty quantification in spatial data and models

GeoAI & Machine Learning

Our GeoAI research focuses on applying artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques to geospatial data and problems. We develop novel deep learning architectures, computer vision methods, and other AI approaches specifically designed for geospatial applications in various domains.

Key Research Topics:

  • Deep learning for satellite image analysis and feature extraction
  • Computer vision techniques for aerial and street-level imagery
  • Transfer learning approaches for geospatial applications
  • Explainable AI methods for geospatial models
  • Integration of physical models with machine learning approaches

Urban Analytics

Our urban analytics research focuses on understanding and modeling urban environments through the lens of geospatial data science. We study urban mobility patterns, growth dynamics, infrastructure planning, and sustainability challenges using diverse data sources and analytical methods.

Key Research Topics:

  • Human mobility and activity patterns in urban areas
  • Urban heat island analysis and mitigation
  • Smart city applications and urban sensing
  • Urban infrastructure planning and optimization

Disaster Management & Resilience

Our disaster management research focuses on developing geospatial solutions for all phases of the disaster management cycle - prevention, preparedness, response, and recovery. We create tools and methodologies for disaster risk assessment, early warning systems, emergency response, and community resilience building.

Key Research Topics:

  • Flood risk assessment and monitoring using remote sensing
  • Machine learning for rapid damage assessment after disasters
  • Multi-hazard vulnerability mapping and analysis
  • Real-time monitoring systems for disaster events
  • Spatial decision support systems for emergency management

Featured Projects:

Geospatial Visualization

Our visualization research focuses on creating innovative techniques and interactive platforms to communicate complex geospatial information effectively to diverse stakeholders. We develop web-based mapping applications, immersive visualization tools, and novel visual analytics approaches for spatial data.

  • 3D visualization of urban environments

  • Cartographic design for effective communication
